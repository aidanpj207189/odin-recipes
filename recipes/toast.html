Toast

Discription

In 2001, Regina Schrambling went on a week long odyssey in search of the best toast recipe. Her ideal here has an intensely flavored break, cooked to the perfect toasted color, and topped with the best peanut butter and jelly.

Ingredients

  • sliced bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. toast one slice of bread
  2. coat one side with peanut butter
  3. on top of the peanut butter, coat with jelly of your choice
  4. cut diagonally
  5. enjoy!
